## I. PURPOSE

This report documents the findings of a kinship placement home study conducted at the residence of Theresa Holloway, 88 Creekside Ln, Greenville, SC. Ms. Holloway is the maternal grandmother of Jaylen Webb (DOB 4/10/2021) and has applied for kinship foster care placement of the child following his removal from the home of his parents on June 12, 2024. This home study is conducted pursuant to S.C. Code Ann. Section 63-7-2320 and DSS Policy 720 (Kinship Care Placement Standards).

## II. APPLICANT BACKGROUND

Theresa Holloway is a 61-year-old retired educator who resides alone at the Greenville address listed above. Ms. Holloway retired from Greenville County School District in 2020 after 32 years of service as an elementary school teacher and reading specialist. She reports a stable income from her state retirement pension and Social Security benefits. She has no other dependents in the home.

Ms. Holloway is the maternal grandmother of Jaylen Webb and reports a close and consistent relationship with the child since birth. She states she has provided regular childcare for Jaylen, typically two to three days per week, and is familiar with his daily routines, dietary preferences, and medical needs. Ms. Holloway expressed a strong desire to provide a safe and stable environment for her grandson during the pendency of the DSS investigation.

## III. HOME DESCRIPTION

The residence at 88 Creekside Ln is a single-family, three-bedroom home in a quiet residential neighborhood in Greenville. The home is owned by Ms. Holloway and has been her primary residence for approximately 18 years.

**General Condition:** The home is clean, well-maintained, and in good structural repair. The interior is orderly and free of hazards. All living areas are adequately furnished.

**Child's Bedroom:** One bedroom has been designated for Jaylen. The room contains an age-appropriate toddler bed with safety rails, a dresser, and a small bookshelf with children's books and toys. The room is on the same floor as Ms. Holloway's bedroom and is adjacent to a full bathroom. The room has adequate lighting and ventilation.

**Kitchen and Dining Area:** The kitchen is clean and stocked with age-appropriate food. Cleaning supplies and medications are stored in a locked cabinet above the refrigerator, out of reach of children.

**Outdoor Space:** The home has a fenced backyard with a small play area. The fence is in good condition and the gate latches securely. No pool or water features are present on the property.

**Safety Features:** Smoke detectors and carbon monoxide detectors are present and functional on each level of the home. Electrical outlets in child-accessible areas are covered. Firearms are not present in the home. Ms. Holloway confirmed she does not own firearms.

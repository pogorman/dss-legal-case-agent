Cases Involving Termination of Parental Rights

Case No. 2024-DR-15-0341 (DSS v. Price, Fifth Judicial Circuit, Richland County, South Carolina)

This case involves the South Carolina Department of Social Services (DSS) seeking termination of parental rights for Crystal Price regarding her minor children, Amari Price and Destiny Price.
The grounds for termination include child neglect, failure to remedy conditions that led to removal, and non-compliance with a court-ordered treatment plan. The biological father, Brandon Price, voluntarily relinquished his parental rights, which was accepted by the court​1​.
Key documents in this case include:
Emergency Removal Order (August 8, 2023)
Order Accepting Voluntary Relinquishment of Parental Rights (November 20, 2023)

Order Following TPR Probable Cause Hearing (April 22, 2024)​1​.
Voluntary Relinquishment of Parental Rights

Brandon Price, the biological father, executed a voluntary relinquishment of parental rights for Amari Price and Destiny Price. The court found the relinquishment was voluntary, knowing, and without coercion, and permanently terminated his parental rights​1​.

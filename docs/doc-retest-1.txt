What evidence contradicts the parents' claim that Jaylen fell from his crib?

The evidence contradicting the parents' claim that Jaylen fell from his crib includes:

Medical Evidence:

The spiral fracture of Jaylen's left humerus is consistent with torsional force (twisting) rather than an impact from a fall.
The bilateral femur fracture is inconsistent with a fall from a standard crib height for a child of Jaylen's age and weight.
Radiographic evidence shows that the two fractures are at different stages of healing, with the femur fracture being 24 to 48 hours older than the humerus fracture, indicating two separate injury events ​1​.
Inconsistencies in Parental Accounts:

Mr. Webb claims he did not re-enter Jaylen's room after putting him to bed at 10:00 PM, while Ms. Holloway's revised statement places Mr. Webb in or near the room at 9:30 PM when she heard a thump and Mr. Webb responded that everything was fine ​1​.
There was an approximate four-and-a-half-hour delay before the parents sought medical attention after the supposed injury event at 9:30 PM, which medical staff found concerning ​1​.
Ms. Holloway initially did not disclose any concerns about Mr. Webb's interactions with Jaylen but later stated that she had witnessed Mr. Webb grab Jaylen roughly by the arm two or three times when frustrated ​1​​​2​.
These points collectively contradict the parents' claim that Jaylen's injuries resulted from a single fall from his crib.

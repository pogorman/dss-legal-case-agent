For 4763 Griscom St (parcel 232452100), the 2004 deed shows a sale price of $146,000 and a “fair market value” of $53,155.20. That $53,155.20 comes from multiplying the then‑assessed value by Philadelphia’s CLR in effect at the time. The math that matches the record is:

Assessed value ≈ $15,680
CLR (2004) ≈ 3.39
$15,680 × 3.39 = $53,155.20
